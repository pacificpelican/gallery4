a photostream website tool in PHP and mySQL

Share your content!

You can set up your own photo sharing tool, and consider what that gives you:

  • Freedom: You can decide what kind of content is appropriate for your web site.
  • Flexibility: The CSS can easily be edited to get the specific look you want.
  • Staying Power: Instead of being up until the service gets shut down, you keep your photos online for as long as you want.

Take a look at the readme file on GitHub--it's pretty straightforward to set up your own photography site wherever you can use PHP and mySQL, which is a lot of web hosts.
